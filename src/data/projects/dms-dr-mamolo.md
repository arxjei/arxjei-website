## Overview

Dr. Ruth Mamolo App is a dental management system designed to streamline clinic
operations. The platform enables business owners to efficiently manage services,
appointments, and patient records, while providing patients with an intuitive
interface to browse services, manage their accounts, and request assistance.

## Key Features

### Admin Dashboard

- **Analytics & Reports** - overview of clinic operations
- **Service Management** - Create and manage dental services and categories
- **Appointment Scheduling** - Handle patient bookings and availability
- **Patient Records** - Secure management of patient information
- **Real-time Notifications** - Stay updated on important clinic activities

### Patient Portal

- **Service Browsing** - Explore available dental services
- **Account Management** - Update personal information and preferences
- **Appointment Booking** - Request and manage appointments
- **Treatment History** - Access past records and invoices

## AI Integration

The system incorporates artificial intelligence powered by **Google Gemini** to
provide intelligent assistance and enhance user experience with custom AI
instructions.

## Technology Stack

**Frontend**

- Tailwind CSS for modern, responsive design
- Alpine.js for lightweight interactivity
- Vanilla JavaScript for custom functionality

**Backend**

- Laravel 11 with Livewire for dynamic components
- MySQL database for reliable data storage
- XAMPP for local development environment

**AI & APIs**

- Google Gemini for AI capabilities
- PayMongo for payment processing
